# The AI Lab for Accountants

A community of practicing accountants, CPAs, EAs, bookkeepers, and firm owners,
learning to use AI in real tax and accounting work. We experiment alongside peers
and share what we build.

Website: https://ailabforaccountants.com

## Member builds

Tools and projects built by Lab members and shared with the community. The Lab
points to them so others can learn and adapt. They are shared by the members who
made them, used under each project's own license and terms, and are not endorsed
as tax advice. The licensed practitioner always reviews, decides, and signs.

### ReasonableComp Studio
Built by Lab member [KaneTaxes](https://github.com/KaneTaxes).

An offline, white-label S-corporation reasonable compensation calculator and
audit-workpaper generator. It documents shareholder-employee reasonable comp using
three approaches (cost, market, income) plus a facts-and-circumstances writeup. It
runs locally with no server, keeps client data on your machine, and produces
tamper-evident exports.

Repo: https://github.com/KaneTaxes/reasonablecomp-studio

Reasonable compensation is a facts-and-circumstances determination. This tool
supports documentation and judgment. It does not replace the practitioner's review
or guarantee an IRS outcome.
